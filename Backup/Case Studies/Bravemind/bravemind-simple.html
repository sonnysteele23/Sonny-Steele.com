BraveMind VR Therapy Platform

Immersive VR exposure therapy applications to help individuals overcome anxiety.

Project Overview

Designed immersive VR exposure therapy applications to help individuals overcome anxiety through controlled, therapeutic virtual environments.

Challenge

Traditional anxiety therapy needed innovative approaches. VR offered new possibilities but required careful UX design for therapeutic effectiveness.

Solution

Created calming, progressive VR environments that allow therapists to gradually expose patients to anxiety triggers in a controlled, safe space.

Key Features

• Therapeutic VR environments
• Progressive exposure controls
• Therapist dashboard tools
• Patient progress tracking

Results

Enabled more effective anxiety treatment through innovative VR therapy experiences, improving patient outcomes and therapy engagement.
