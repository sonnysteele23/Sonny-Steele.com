Weather Channel Running App

Designing intelligent running companion app that integrates real-time weather data with route planning, safety alerts, and performance tracking to optimize outdoor running experiences in any weather condition.

Design Strategy

Combining weather intelligence with running performance to create safer, smarter outdoor experiences

1

Weather Integration

Deep integration with Weather Channel data to provide real-time conditions, forecasts, and weather-based route recommendations for optimal running conditions.

2

Safety Alerts

Proactive safety alert system for severe weather, air quality, and visibility conditions with alternative route suggestions and timing recommendations.

3

Route Optimization

Intelligent route planning that considers weather patterns, terrain, and user preferences to suggest optimal running paths and timing.

4

Performance Tracking

Comprehensive running analytics that correlate weather conditions with performance metrics to help runners understand environmental impact on their training.
